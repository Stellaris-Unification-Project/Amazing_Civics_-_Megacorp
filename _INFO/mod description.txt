A pack of 12 Civics and 1 Origin, all designed to open up more playstyles for Megacorporations. The focus of this mod is to create interesting options that provide players with new and fresh gameplay.
                                                                                                                  
This mod works with Version 3.1.* of the game. 

[b]Available Languages:[/b]
English
Chinese  (Translated by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561198254983643]Rui_MS[/url]) (Not entirely up to date)
Spanish  (Translated by [url=https://steamcommunity.com/profiles/76561198806487951/]Alexander[/url])

[img]https://i.imgur.com/E6Zj7Fl.png[/img]

This Mod was designed with maximum compatibility in mind. It does not override or alter any game files and as a result, it should be compatible with just about any other mod.
